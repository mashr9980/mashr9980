## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm **Muhammad Aashir Tariq** — an AI Engineer and SaaS architect based in Pakistan. I build production AI systems end-to-end: from LLM agents, RAG pipelines, and fine-tuned models to the full-stack apps that ship them. I hold a **Master's in Artificial Intelligence** from FAST-NUCES (thesis: multi-modal toxic span detection for low-resource languages using **VGG-19, OpenSMILE, and XLM-RoBERTa**) and a Bachelor's in Computer Science from COMSATS (FYP: Urdu voice-command recognition with MFCC, mel spectrograms, and deep learning).

Today I'm the **Founder & CEO of [Afnexis](https://www.linkedin.com/in/muhammad-aashir-tariq-314769405/)** and **Senior Full-Stack AI Engineer at My Medical Records.ai**, where I lead development of a HIPAA-compliant SaaS platform with AI-powered medical summaries and a health Q&A chatbot. Previously I led ML engineers at ESAP AI in Saudi Arabia, building automated call centers with real-time STT/RAG/TTS, and shipped MLOps pipelines at Hosting Controller Inc.

- 🔭 Currently building **My Medical Records.ai** (HIPAA-compliant AI health platform) and growing **Afnexis**
